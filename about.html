Who are we?

Founded in 2017, Brewed Beet is one of Vancouver’s best online resources for discovering the freshest seasonal offerings from local breweries and restaurants. In addition to offering expert advice and reviews on our favorite seasonal selections, we also provide easy recipes to try from home. Find out what’s hot and what’s not, when the seasons switch. Currently consisting of 5 full-time contributors, the Brewed Beet is always one beat ahead when it comes to seasonal brews and bites

We always give our honest opinion, no exceptions. Of course, we try to be fair; but if something needs to be said, we’ll say it. This is not to say our opinion should be valued above others. Food is a very personal thing, and what some people call a delicious meal, others will call trash. Some might think we do this for the free food, gifts and invitations to lavish grand openings. While those things are nice, we do this because we’re passionate about food and drinks. There will always be good and bad, nobody is perfect. We’ll do our best to let you know which is which.

Regardless of the season, check with us to make sure you’re getting the latest and greatest Vancouver has to offer. When it comes to finding fresh local flavors, the brewed beet is unbeatable.
